Welcome

Chairman, Korea International Leaders Congress (KILC)

Young-Kook Jung

Welcome to the Korea International Leaders Congress (KILC) website.

The Korea International Leaders Congress is an organization established to unite 7.5 million overseas Koreans and domestic Korean people, establish ethnic identity, and consolidate the capabilities of our nation scattered around the world.

Our compatriots who left their homeland and migrated to countries around the world amid the upheavals of 20th-century history now play pivotal roles in their respective countries and are promoting the excellence of the Korean people worldwide.

KILC's top priority is to strengthen and support ethnic education and historical-cultural education so that overseas Koreans can maintain their ethnic identity and live with pride.

We also support the stable settlement of compatriots wishing to return home and provide advance education and information to help our young people successfully settle abroad.

Through the Global Korean Network (GKN), we are building a platform where Korean leaders at home and abroad can cooperate closely and achieve mutual prosperity, further strengthening the global competitiveness of the Korean people.

We will strive to promote the values of the '9 Haan' - Hangeul, Hansik, Hanbok, Hanok, Hanji, Hanak, Hanbang, Hansang, and Hankyore Spirit - to the world, and lay the foundation for the '1 Tong' (unification) of peaceful reunification of the Korean Peninsula.

We ask for your interest and participation.
Thank you.

Chairman, Korea International Leaders Congress (KILC)

Young-Kook Jung
