Korea International Leaders Congress Charter

Chapter 1: General Provisions

Article 1 (Name)
This organization shall be called "Korea International Leaders Congress" (hereinafter referred to as "KILC").

Article 2 (Purpose)
The purpose of this organization is to establish the ethnic identity of 7.5 million overseas Koreans, operate the Global Korean Network (GKN), and promote mutual prosperity and development of Koreans worldwide.

Article 3 (Office Location)
The headquarters of KILC shall be located in Seoul, Korea, with regional branches established in major cities worldwide.

Chapter 2: Business

Article 4 (Business Activities)
To achieve its purpose, KILC conducts the following activities:

  • 1. Strengthening ethnic education and historical-cultural education
  • 2. Operating the Global Korean Network (GKN)
  • 3. Supporting settlement of returning compatriots
  • 4. Providing information and education for overseas expansion
  • 5. Promoting 9 Haan (Korean cultural values) worldwide
  • 6. Contributing to peaceful reunification of the Korean Peninsula
  • 7. Hosting World Korean Leaders Convention
  • 8. Other activities necessary to achieve the organization's purpose

Chapter 3: Membership

Article 5 (Membership Categories)
KILC membership consists of:

  • 1. Regular Members: Korean leaders dedicated to ethnic prosperity
  • 2. Honorary Members: Individuals who have made significant contributions
  • 3. Supporting Members: Organizations and individuals supporting KILC

Article 6 (Membership Qualifications)
Regular members include:

  • Current and former Korean Association presidents
  • PUAC members
  • World-OKTA members
  • KOWIN members
  • Ethnic educators and cultural leaders
  • Other Korean leaders approved by the Board

Chapter 4: Organization

Article 7 (Organizational Structure)
KILC shall have the following organizational structure:

  • 1. General Assembly
  • 2. Board of Directors
  • 3. Advisory Council
  • 4. Regional Branches
  • 5. Special Committees

Article 8 (Officers)
KILC shall have the following officers:

  • 1. Chairman: 1 person
  • 2. Vice Chairmen: Multiple persons
  • 3. Secretary General: 1 person
  • 4. Directors: Multiple persons
  • 5. Auditors: 2 persons

Chapter 5: Meetings

Article 9 (General Assembly)
The General Assembly shall be held annually to approve:

  • Business plans and budgets
  • Business reports and financial statements
  • Election of officers
  • Charter amendments
  • Other important matters

Chapter 6: Finance

Article 10 (Financial Resources)
The finances of KILC shall consist of:

  • Membership fees
  • Donations and sponsorships
  • Government subsidies
  • Revenue from business activities
  • Other income

Chapter 7: Supplementary Provisions

Article 11 (Charter Amendments)
This charter may be amended by a two-thirds majority vote of the General Assembly.

Article 12 (Effective Date)
This charter shall take effect from the date of adoption.
