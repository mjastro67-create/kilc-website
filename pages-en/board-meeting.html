KILC Board Meeting

Executive Board Decision-Making Body

Quarterly Meetings

Venue TBA

Capacity: 200 people

Assembly Overview

The 11th KILC General Assembly in 2026 is an important meeting to review and approve the 2025 business settlement report and the 2026 business plan and budget.

KILC members, board members, and stakeholders from around the world will attend to review the achievements of the past year and discuss the direction of business for the new year.

Main Agenda

  • Opening and National Anthem
  • Chairman's Address
  • 2025 Business Settlement Report
    • Major Business Implementation Results
    • Financial Settlement Report
    • Audit Report
  • Deliberation and Resolution of 2026 Business Plan
    • World Korean Leaders Convention Plan
    • Overseas Korean Education Support Project
    • 9 Haan 1 Tong Cultural Promotion Project
    • Global Korean Network Establishment
  • Deliberation and Resolution of 2026 Budget
  • Other Agenda and Discussion
  • Closing

Participation Information

Participants: KILC Members, Board Members, and Stakeholders
